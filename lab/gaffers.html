Frequently asked questions

Can it power LED walls and video playback?

Yes. 10kW continuous output handles full LED walls, video playback systems, theatrical lighting, and audio simultaneously.

How long between charges?

8-12 hours typical runtime for activations. Varies based on load. Fast charging (1 hour with 60A) enables battery rotation for multi-day events.

What if we need more power than one unit?

Stack multiple LAB SEVEN units or add generator backup placed away from guest areas. We help you calculate power needs based on your activation specs.

Do we need special insurance?

No. LFP chemistry is insurance-friendly and meets venue safety requirements. Easier insurance approval than generators.

Can we use it indoors?

Yes. Zero emissions make it safe for occupied indoor spaces including hotel ballrooms, retail environments, and galleries.

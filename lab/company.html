Our Story

01

Started in Film & Entertainment

[PLACEHOLDER: Founded to solve portable power problems for film and TV productions. Learned hard lessons about reliability, fleet management, and user behavior in high-stakes environments where power failure means production shutdown.]

02

Shifted to Municipal Programs

[PLACEHOLDER: As California regulations on small off-road engines tightened, we saw opportunity to apply production-proven fleet management to city operations. Open-streets festivals, farmers markets, and park rentals face the same power challenges we'd already solved.]

03

Built for Public-Sector Scale

Municipal programs require different thinking than equipment rentals. Cross-department coordination, procurement compliance, grant alignment, policy outcomes. We design programs that fit how cities actually work, not how we wish they worked.

Values & Approach

Reliability Over Hype

We don't promise emergency backup power or grid independence. We promise farmers markets run on time, bounce houses stay inflated, and festivals don't lose sound mid-set. Boring reliability is our product.

Safety-First Hardware

LiFePO₄ chemistry for thermal stability in crowded public spaces. Cell-level monitoring and geofencing. UL-listed components. We design for deployments near children, food, and residential neighbors – not just industrial sites.

Vendor-Managed Service

Cities don't want boxes of batteries. They want generators gone from farmers markets. We sell uptime and outcomes, not hardware. Fleet management software, maintenance workflows, SLAs, and on-site support are the product.

Public-Sector Pragmatism

[PLACEHOLDER: We understand RFP cycles, grant timelines, Council approval processes, and cross-department politics. Our team has municipal experience and speaks the language of city staff, not just battery engineers.]

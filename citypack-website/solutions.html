Markets, Events & Parks Programs

Farmers Markets & Street Vendor Power

City-managed battery fleet replaces vendor-owned generators. Two models: city check-out system or vendor drop-off/pickup each market day. 4-6 hour runtime for lights, freezers, and food prep. Behavior-change support to transition vendors off generators permanently.

City-Sponsored Events & Festivals

From single-stage concerts to multi-block open streets. City designates single battery provider during pilot; event organizers contact vendor directly. Delivery, setup, on-site support, teardown included. Stages, sound, lighting, food vendors, security infrastructure – all powered quietly.

Park Rentals & Bounce Houses

Emission-free power for bounce house blowers and party equipment. 4+ hour runtime with multi-battery configurations for full-day events. Vendor works with city-approved jumper suppliers and park permit process. Keep kids' parties quiet and fume-free.

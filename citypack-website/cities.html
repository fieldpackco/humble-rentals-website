What a Two-Year Pilot Includes

Phase 1: Planning & Stakeholder Alignment

[PLACEHOLDER: Cross-department discovery, program scope definition, budget sizing aligned with grant constraints. Support for RFP development if needed. Target scenarios identified: which markets, which parks, which events.]

Phase 2: Infrastructure & Field Crew Rollout

[PLACEHOLDER: Charging circuit installation in city yard. Field crew portable units deployed with staff training. Baseline data collection on current generator usage and costs across all four scenarios.]

Phase 3: Market, Park & Event Conversion

[PLACEHOLDER: First generator-free farmers market. Pilot bounce house battery deployments at weekend park rentals. Support for first city-sponsored festival powered by batteries. Vendor outreach and behavior-change support.]

Phase 4: Measurement & Policy Recommendations

[PLACEHOLDER: Data analysis: emissions avoided, generator-hours replaced, vendor participation, community feedback. Staff support for permanent policy development: event permit requirements, park rental clean power rules, vendor transition timelines.]
